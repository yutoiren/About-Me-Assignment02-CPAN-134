Cycling

        I enjoy riding my bike because it allows me to get from one place to another pretty quickly. I also enjoy how it allows the rider to go at their own pace, increasing or decreasing their speed as they wish. I do enjoy going on walks as well, but I find that riding a bike is a lot more exciting as you get to take in all of your surroundings and nature at a faster pace than you would on foot.
